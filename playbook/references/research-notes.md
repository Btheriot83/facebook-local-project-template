## Practical Conclusion

For local businesses, the winning Facebook model is:
- original proof-first Reels for discovery
- trust-building feed posts with local specificity
- lightweight Stories for recency
- limited CTA/link posts
- disciplined comment/message follow-up
- no lazy watermarked reposting
